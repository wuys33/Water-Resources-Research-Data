the values of PN risk level in FS1_PN risk level.dat, FS2_PN risk level.dat, FS3_PN risk level.dat and FS4_PN risk level.datmean that:
PN:
Value=1, low risk level,  PN<0.3 m^2/s
Value=2, moderate risk level,  0.3≤PN<0.7 m^2/s
Value=3, significant risk level,  0.7≤PN<1.0 m^2/s
Value=4, high risk level,  PN≥1.0 m^2/s

And the values of PN risk level only show valuse in the range of the Macau Peninsula
